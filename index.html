OPS Plan Overview

Introduction

Help Desk Supervisor | IT Support Leader

Reason for Plan

Help Desk Operations Plan for the upcoming fiscal year. This plan focuses on improving efficiency, response times, customer satisfaction through measurable goals as well as strategic improvements.

Mission

Deliver fast, effective IT support to enhance productivity.

Vision

Develop a future-ready help desk that provide efficient support.

SMART Goals and Benefits

SMART Goal Benefit
Reduce response time by 10% by year-end. Faster service improves productivity.
Increase first contact resolution rate by 10% by year end. Users resolve issues on first contact.
Decrease incident resolution time by 10% by year-end. Quicker fixes enhance user experience.
Increase self-service effectiveness by 10% by year-end. Self-service reduces workload.
Decrease backlog by 15% by year-end. Less backlog means fewer delays.
